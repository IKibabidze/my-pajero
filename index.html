Super Select 4WD-II System

This 2010 Pajero GLS is equipped with the versatile SS4-II drivetrain, featuring four distinct driving modes:

  • 2H (2WD High Range): Rear-wheel drive for fuel efficiency on dry, paved roads.
  • 4H (4WD High Range): Full-time 4WD that can be used on any surface (even asphalt) for better traction.
  • 4HLc (4WD High Range with Locked Center Diff): Best for rugged terrain, snow, or sand; distributes power 50:50 between axles.
  • 4LLc (4WD Low Range with Locked Center Diff): Maximum torque for extreme off-roading, steep climbs, or deep mud.
  • Rear Diff Lock (R/D Lock): Includes a separate factory-installed rear differential lock for ultimate 4x4 capability.

Active Stability & Traction Control (ASTC)

The 2010 Pajero 4 features advanced electronic aids that work alongside the mechanical 4WD:

  • Traction Control: Automatically brakes a spinning wheel to transfer torque to the wheels with grip.
  • Stability Control (ASC): Prevents skidding by reducing engine power and braking individual wheels during sharp turns.
  • ASC OFF Switch: A dedicated button to disable stability control. This is essential when stuck in deep mud or sand where you need consistent wheel spin and the full power of the 3.8 V6.
  • Off-Road Mode: When the R/D Lock is engaged, the ABS and Stability Control are automatically adjusted or deactivated to prioritize raw mechanical traction.
